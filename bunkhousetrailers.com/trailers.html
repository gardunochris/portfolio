Our Bunkhouse Trailers

Choose from three trailer sizes to accommodate your crew. Every unit includes climate control, power outlets at each bunk, locking storage and sturdy entry steps. Generator packages are available for off-grid deployments.

8-Station Bunkhouse Trailer

Ideal for smaller response teams, this trailer houses eight climate-controlled bunks with LED lighting and personal shelving. Compact size makes it easy to position in tight areas.

10-Station Bunkhouse Trailer

Our most popular model balances capacity and maneuverability. Ten comfortable bunks with heating and A/C keep crews rested during extended operations.

12-Station Bunkhouse Trailer

Best suited for large incidents, this spacious unit features twelve bunks, central HVAC, and individual power outlets so your crew can recharge gear and themselves.
