About Us

BunkhouseTrailers.com provides nationwide short and long-term bunkhouse trailer rentals specializing in disaster and emergency response. From fire and hurricane relief to support for homeless encampments, our fleet is ready for rapid deployment. Based in Fresno, California, our partnership network serves the entire USA with government and military collaborations.

What We Do

  • Same day emergency deployment
  • Short and long-term rental solutions
  • Support for disasters, events, and remote operations

Nationwide Service

Our trailer hub is in Fresno, CA and we deploy anywhere in the continental United States.
